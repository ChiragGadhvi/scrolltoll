# 🛡️ ScrollToll - Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-03-27

ScrollToll is a screen time tracking tool designed to prioritize user privacy above everything else.

---

### 1. 🔒 100% Local Processing
Everything ScrollToll does happens on your physical device. We do not use any external servers, cloud databases, or tracking scripts. Your screen time data is used ONLY within the app to calculate your daily time-value score.

### 2. 📵 No Data Collection
- **We do NOT collect** your name, email, or any personal identity information.
- **We do NOT read** your app content, messages, or keystrokes.
- **We do NOT send** your app usage metrics to any third parties, including our own team.

### 3. 📊 What Permissions We Use
To function correctly, ScrollToll requires the **Usage Access** permission. This is a standard Android permission that allows the app to see how many minutes you spent on other apps. This permission is handled entirely on-device and is never shared externally.

### 4. 🗄️ Database Storage
We use the **Hive** storage engine locally on your phone to store your "Daily Savings History" so you can see your own progress. This local database is encrypted by Android and was built for privacy. If you delete the app, this data is wiped from your device.

### 5. 📧 Contact Us
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer via the official Google Play developer page.

---

**Summary:** Your data never leaves your pocket.
